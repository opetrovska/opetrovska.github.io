About

Thanks for stopping by my little corner of the internet! I'm so glad you are here.

Let me introduce myself. I work at Swansea University, where I get to share my passion for computing and software engineering with amazing students. It's incredibly rewarding work, but there's so much more to my story!

Beyond teaching, I enjoy managing projects, organising events, and researching topics related to computing education, generative AI, and logic. When I am not busy with those activities, you are likely to find me painting or visiting yet another botanical garden.
